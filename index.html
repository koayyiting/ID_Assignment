About Us

What is this website about?
This Website is made to introduce our lovely cats to Singaporeans.
On this website, we will be able to Adopt, Donate and Volunteer!

How We Started:
We started from volunteering events when we are young, after school hours, every weekend. Soon, we started looking at ways to help the stray cats just around our neighbourhood. After a few months, we gathered like-minded people around Singapore to start an organisation called KaT. We hoped to give our cats a sense of belonging and a warm-hearted place for them to stay.
